Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data Fix My Speaker ("we," "us," or "our") collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you visit fix-my-speaker.org.

1. Information We Collect

Analytics data: We collect standard, anonymous web analytics including pages visited, approximate geographic region, device type, and browser type. This data is aggregated and never linked to individual users.

Contact form submissions: If you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message to respond to your inquiry.

What we do not collect: We do not collect audio data from your device. The Web Audio API used by Fix My Speaker operates entirely in your browser — no audio is transmitted to our servers. We do not collect device identifiers, serial numbers, or personal information beyond what you voluntarily submit via the contact form.

2. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without loss of core tool functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to contact form inquiries
  • To understand general usage patterns and improve the website
  • To monitor site performance and diagnose technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to load web typography. Your browser may contact Google's servers to retrieve font files; Google's privacy policy governs this interaction. We do not embed advertising networks, social tracking pixels, or other third-party scripts beyond analytics and fonts.

5. Data Retention

Contact form data is retained for up to 12 months or until the inquiry is fully resolved. Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 26 months before deletion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold about you. Contact us at hello@fix-my-speaker.org to make a request.

7. Children's Privacy

Fix My Speaker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at hello@fix-my-speaker.org or via our contact form.
