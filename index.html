Step by Step

How to Fix My Speaker

Follow these steps for the best results. Run both modes 2-3 times for heavily water-exposed devices.

1

Wipe Your Device

Use a dry cloth to remove any visible water on your phone before starting. This prevents more water from entering the speaker.

2

Max Your Volume

Turn device volume all the way up. Maximum volume ensures the strongest possible sound waves reach the speaker diaphragm.

3

Place on Flat Surface

Lay your phone face-up on a flat, stable surface while the tool works. Gravity helps drain water downward away from components.

4

Run Sound Wave Mode

Press the eject button and wait for the progress bar to reach 100%. Frequencies shift every 2 seconds for full coverage.

5

Switch to Vibration

Select Vibration mode and run again. This dislodges any remaining moisture and deeper debris from the speaker mesh.

6

Test & Repeat

Play some audio to check improvement. Repeat the full 2-mode cycle 2-3 times for heavily water-damaged speakers.
