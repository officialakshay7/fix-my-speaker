🔊 Service Description

Fix My Speaker is a free, browser-based tool that uses the Web Audio API to generate sound frequencies intended to help eject water and dislodge dust from phone and device speakers. The Service operates entirely within your web browser and requires no download, installation, or account registration.

The Service provides:

  • Sound Wave Mode — cycles through multiple audio frequencies to vibrate speaker components
  • Vibration Mode — generates broadband noise to dislodge debris from speaker mesh
  • A progress indicator showing the duration of each cleaning cycle
⚠️

Fix My Speaker is a first-response tool, not a professional repair service. It is designed to help with minor water and dust exposure. It cannot repair physical speaker damage, corrosion, or hardware failure. For severe damage, always consult a professional repair service.
