📋 Overview

Fix My Speaker ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website at https://fix-my-speaker.org/. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our speaker cleaning tool.

The short version: we collect almost nothing. Fix My Speaker is a browser-based utility that runs entirely on your device. The tool uses your device's speaker to play sound — it does not upload files, record audio, or transmit any data to our servers.

💡

Plain English: When you press the button, sound plays through your speaker. That's it. Nothing is sent anywhere. Nothing is stored about you.

📥 Data We Collect

When you visit Fix My Speaker, our web hosting provider may automatically log the following standard server data for security and operational purposes:

  • Your IP address (used only for rate limiting and abuse prevention)
  • The date and time of your visit
  • The pages you requested (URL paths)
  • Your browser type and operating system (user agent string)
  • The referring website, if applicable

This data is collected automatically by our hosting infrastructure, is not linked to any personal identity, and is retained for a maximum of 30 days for security purposes only. We do not use this data for advertising or profiling.

If you use our Contact Us form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message). This is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is never shared with third parties.

🚫 What We Don't Collect

We want to be explicitly clear about what we do not collect or access:

  • We do not access your microphone or record any audio
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information unless you contact us voluntarily
  • We do not track your location or GPS data
  • We do not read or access any files on your device
  • We do not create user profiles or accounts
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising targeting
  • We do not share data with data brokers
🔒

The Web Audio API used by our tool only outputs sound — it does not input or record audio. Your microphone is never accessed.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

Fix My Speaker uses minimal, functional-only browser storage:

  • localStorage key fms-theme — Saves your light/dark mode preference so it persists across visits. This is stored only in your own browser and is never transmitted to us.

We do not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party cookies from ad networks
  • Session cookies that identify you
  • Cross-site tracking pixels

You can clear your browser's localStorage at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will simply reset your theme preference to the default (light mode).

🔗 Third-Party Services

Fix My Speaker uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — We load the Sora and DM Sans fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests these fonts, Google may log your IP address. You can review Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Web Hosting Provider — Our hosting provider stores standard server logs as described in the "Data We Collect" section above.

We do not integrate any advertising networks, social media trackers, or affiliate tracking scripts on our website.

💡

To avoid Google Fonts loading externally, you can self-host the fonts. This would mean zero third-party requests from our site whatsoever.

📊 Analytics

We may use privacy-focused analytics to understand how many people visit our site and which pages are most useful. If we do use analytics:

  • Data is aggregated and anonymised — individual users are never identified
  • IP addresses are anonymised before any processing
  • No cross-site tracking is performed
  • Data is used only to improve the tool, never for advertising

We do not use Google Analytics' default configuration which sets tracking cookies. Any analytics we use are configured to be fully cookie-free and GDPR-compliant.

👶 Children's Privacy

Fix My Speaker is a general utility tool suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since our tool does not require any account registration or personal data submission to function, children can use Fix My Speaker safely without providing any personal information.

If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us via our contact form, please contact us at help@fix-my-speaker.org and we will promptly delete it.

🛡️ Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the limited data we do process:

  • All pages are served over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Server logs are stored securely and automatically purged after 30 days
  • Contact form submissions are transmitted over encrypted connections
  • We do not store payment information of any kind (the tool is free)

Because we collect so little data, the risk surface is minimal. The best privacy protection is simply not collecting data in the first place — and that is our approach.

⚖️ Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data under laws such as the GDPR (European Union) or CCPA (California). These include:

  • Right to access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to correction — request that we correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to object — object to certain types of processing
  • Right to portability — request your data in a portable format

Since we collect almost no personal data, most of these rights are satisfied by default. If you submitted a contact form message and wish to have it deleted, simply email us at help@fix-my-speaker.org.

We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

  • Significant changes will be noted prominently on the homepage
  • Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this page periodically. This policy was last updated on April 7, 2025.
